  • 59

    A2

    A2 cars can only operate as lead or trail cars in a train consist. They have an operator's cab, automatic train operating equipment, and two-way communications system. Seats 60 customers.

  • 389

    B2

    B2 cars can only operate in the middle of a train consist. They do not have a cab nor do they control the operation of the train. Can, however, carry the same customer load as an A2.

  • 150

    C1

    C1 cars can operate as either lead, trail or in the middle of a train consist. They are equipped with an operator's compartment, automatic train control equipment and a communications system.

  • 80

    C2

    C2 cars are nearly identical to C1 cars. Although C1 and C2 cars have slightly less seating capacity, 56, they give BART flexibility to change train size without rerouting to a storage yard.
